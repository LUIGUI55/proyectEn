👗 Traditional Clothing

For Women - "Saral"

The traditional outfit consists of:

  • Sleek bodysuit in metallic silver and holographic accents
  • High-tech LED-embedded harness with neon blue and purple lines
  • Futuristic boots with integrated glow elements
  • Luminescent arm cuffs with digital patterns
  • Holographic visor headpiece during festivities

For Men - "Kortaj"

The traditional male attire includes:

  • Oversized cargo pants with multiple straps and patches
  • Distressed leather jacket with chain details
  • Heavy boots with metal accents
  • Band tees and vintage graphic clothing
  • Chains, rings and bold accessories for formal occasions

🎖️ The President

Isac Mírov Castañeda

Age: 30 years old

Description

Isac Mírov is a man of medium height, with short black hair and gray streaks that he proudly displays. Of robust build, he has hazel eyes and a warm smile that contrasts with his analytical gaze.

He was a student of fine arts and music, but years later he dedicated himself to music. He is known for his versatility when singing. He usually wears tailored suits in light, fresh tones, but always wears a brooch with Obscria's national flower (the Snow Flower) as a symbol of his roots.

On weekends, when he can, he escapes to his cabin in the northern mountains to disconnect a bit from his city life and work as president.
